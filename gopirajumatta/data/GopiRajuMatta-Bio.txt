GOPI RAJU MATTA (Student Member, IEEE) is currently pursuing his Ph.D. with the Computational Imaging Lab, Department of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras, Chennai, India. He received his B.Tech.degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Rajiv Gandhi University of Knowledge Technologies (RGUKT), IIIT Nuzvid, India, in 2015, and the M.Tech. degree from the Indian Institute of Engineering Science and Technology(IIEST), Shibpur, India, in 2017. His research interests include computer vision, 3D reconstruction, Neural Radiance Fields, and 3D Gaussian Splatting.
